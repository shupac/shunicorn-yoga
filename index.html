About Shu

I'm a certified Forrest Yoga teacher with over 300 hrs of training. In my practice and teaching, I focus on building strength and releasing tension.

I began meditation and yoga in 2004 to recover from a physical and mental breakdown caused by stress. My path of inner discovery led me to Forrest Yoga in 2015 and I felt an immediate difference in this practice. Forrest Yoga has helped me release toxic emotions in my body that have prevented me from realizing my fullest potential and connecting to love.

Through teaching yoga, I hope to help others find more ease and enjoyment in their lives while still working towards their goals. I believe that external achievement goes hand-in-hand with internal growth--that the process of pursuing our goals strengthens us while our emotions are the key to unlocking physical manifestations. I see yoga as an effective and safe way to practice finding alignment between these two worlds.

Forrest Yoga is an intelligent and detailed style of yoga developed to heal the body of injuries and to release emotions. Poses are held longer with precise attention to breath and alignment. While physically challenging, Forrest Yoga is accessible for people with all ability levels and injuries.

The real richness of Forrest Yoga lies in one's inner experience. Classes are guided by intentions of personal feeling and exploration. Caring hands-on assists help students connect deeper into the body, emotions, and spirit.
